The Best Movies According to Jolene

My Top Movies of All Time

Oppenheimer

I recently saw this film and it instantly became one of my favorites.

Bridget Jones Diary

Perhaps this film doesn't stand the test of time, but I have related so much with this quirky singleton.

All About Eve

Such a classic tale, an aging star crosses paths with an ambitious ingenue. Bette Davis has an onscreen presence that is larger than life and doesn't age.
